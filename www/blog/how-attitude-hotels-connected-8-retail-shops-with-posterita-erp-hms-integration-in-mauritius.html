How Attitude Hotels Connected 8 Retail Shops with Posterita ERP + HMS Integration in Mauritius

Nina Brilane · September 17, 2025

Behind the Counter: A Complex Retail Setup

Attitude Hotels operates 8 boutique-style shops across its Mauritian properties, offering locally inspired skincare, souvenirs, and lifestyle products managed by an external tenant.

The operation faced significant challenges:

  • Sales were not connected to the hotel's Infor HMS, requiring manual guest billing
  • Stock visibility across locations was inconsistent
  • Guest versus non-guest transactions were not properly tracked
  • Sales data required manual formatting before Power BI delivery
  • Barcode labels were inconsistent between locations

The Breakthrough: Posterita ERP + HMS Integration

Real-Time Cloud Access

Both the tenant and hotel HQ can now access sales, stock, and performance data instantly from any device.

HMS Integration with Infor

Sales are linked directly to guest room numbers with real-time verification, eliminating manual billing processes.

Guest vs. Non-Guest Transaction Segmentation

Guest purchases are automatically posted to rooms, while walk-in sales are routed to external accounts.

Sales Data Delivered to Power BI

Nightly automatic export in the required format keeps analytics dashboards up to date without manual work.

Barcode Labeling Tools

Custom uniform barcode generation across all locations ensures consistency and reduces scanning errors.
