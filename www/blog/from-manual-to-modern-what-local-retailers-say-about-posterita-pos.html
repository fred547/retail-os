The Reality of Manual Retail Operations

Across Mauritius — from Port Louis to Flacq, Rose Belle, and Curepipe — retailers have long relied on pen-and-paper systems to manage sales, stock, and daily cash. But as customer expectations evolve and competition rises, manual operations create real bottlenecks:

  • Slow checkout processes frustrate customers and reduce revenue during peak hours.
  • Inventory discrepancies become common due to handwritten records and missing barcode systems.
  • End-of-day reporting is time-consuming, error-prone, and stressful.
  • Scaling across multiple locations feels nearly impossible without visibility across stores.

That's where Posterita comes in.
