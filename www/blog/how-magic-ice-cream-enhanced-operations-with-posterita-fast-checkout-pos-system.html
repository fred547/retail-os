How Magic Ice Cream Enhanced Operations with Posterita Fast Checkout POS System

Nina Brilane · January 31, 2025

The Challenge

Magic Ice Cream, located in Beau Bassin, Mauritius, operates a fast-food establishment offering burgers, ice creams, milkshakes, crepes, pasta, wraps, paninis, and waffles. Owner Ashfaq R. confronted several operational obstacles:

  • Manual order communication to kitchen increased error risk
  • Order accuracy issues during peak hours affected customer satisfaction
  • Daily till reconciliation proved challenging and error-prone
  • Inability to monitor operations remotely when away from the store

The Solution: Posterita Fast Checkout POS System

Five key features were implemented to address each challenge:

1. Online POS Access

Real-time business monitoring from any location. Owner Ashfaq can now check sales, review orders, and monitor performance from anywhere.

2. Kitchen Printing

Automated order transmission to the kitchen, reducing errors. No more shouting orders or writing tickets by hand.

3. Product Categorization

Quick, accurate order processing during busy periods. Products organized by category for fast selection at the counter.

4. Fast Checkout

Streamlined transaction interface minimizing wait times. Every second counts during peak hours.

5. Cash Control

Enhanced till reconciliation and theft prevention. End-of-day cash management became straightforward and reliable.

The Results

30%
Faster Service Times
99%
Order Accuracy
  • 30% faster service times: Kitchen printing streamlined order preparation
  • 99% order accuracy: Automated workflows eliminated manual errors
  • Simplified till reconciliation: End-of-day cash management became quicker and more reliable
  • Real-time business oversight: Remote POS access allowed better monitoring from anywhere
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction: Faster checkout led to more repeat customers

Hardware Configuration

Magic Ice Cream's setup included:

  • Custom tablet for the POS interface
  • Cash drawer and thermal printer from Posterita
  • Professional installation services

With the right tools in place, Magic Ice Cream transformed from manual processes to a streamlined, efficient operation — proving that even a small fast-food outlet can benefit enormously from modern POS technology.
