Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Posterita POS for free?

Yes! Posterita offers a generous Free Plan with 1 user, 1 terminal, 1 store, unlimited products, unlimited orders, and unlimited customers. You get POS with offline mode, customer management, modifiers, CSV export, kitchen printing, and basic inventory & loyalty — with 7-day sales history. Receipts show “Powered by Posterita.” No credit card required. Paid plans start from $7/mo with regional pricing.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on your business location. We adjust prices to be affordable for businesses everywhere — same features, same quality, tailored to your market. Prices are shown in USD and displayed automatically based on your location.

How do I get more users or terminals?

Each additional store doubles your users and terminals. No per-user or per-terminal add-ons — just add stores as you grow. Use our pricing calculator to estimate your exact monthly cost.

What happens when I reach plan limits?

Products, orders, and customers are unlimited on every plan — even Free. The only scaling limits are users and terminals per store. Need more? Add extra stores — each store doubles your users & terminals. No per-user or per-terminal fees. Cloud retention: Free (7 days), Starter (1 year), Growth (3 years), Business (5 years).

Does Posterita work offline?

Absolutely. Every POS operation works without internet — on Android and the Desktop PWA. Transactions sync automatically when connectivity returns. You'll never miss a sale.

What platforms does Posterita run on?

Posterita runs on Android (tablets/phones), Windows, Mac, and Linux as an installable PWA, and any modern browser for the web console. KDS works on dedicated Android tablets over LAN.

How does Posterita ensure security?

Posterita uses blind till reconciliation, role-based access control, audit trails for every transaction, PIN-based device locking, terminal device locking (one terminal = one device), and encrypted cloud storage. QR badge scanning provides staff authentication with anti-fraud measures.

Can I get a custom enterprise solution?

Yes! The Enterprise plan provides ERP integration (Xero built-in, QuickBooks coming soon), custom reporting, unlimited terminals and stores, premium support with a dedicated account manager, and custom data retention policies. Contact us at support@posterita.com.
